# Description

A Raspberry Pi 5 running [Ultrafeeder](https://github.com/sdr-enthusiasts/docker-adsb-ultrafeeder) with a SDR (Software-defined radio) that is connected to an antenna on the roof. 

The data captured by our flight feeder can be viewed here
https://flightfeeder.pawprint.space/
