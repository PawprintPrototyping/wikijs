# Miscellaneous Tools

## Large Tools

- **Woodworking bench.** Includes the SawStop tablesaw listed below, a router, a hand plane, a pull saw, and various woodworking related hand tools.

## Saws
- **SawStop tablesaw.** Lives in the woodworking bench.
  - This saw **must** be operated with the dust collection cyclone and vacuum.
  - **DO NOT** saw laser-cut materials or materials with metal fragments in them without disabling the saw-stop feature. Carbonized laser-cut edges or metal bits will trigger the saw-stop, requiring a $300 replacement part.
- **Ryobi chop saw.** Lives on the big red shelves. Chops things by sawing.
- **Makita track saw.** The saw lives in a blue Makita box on the big red shelves. The track is usually nearby. If clamping the track down, be careful not to hit the clamps with the saw (again).
- **Green Laguna Tools band saw.** Lives on the wire metal cart for some reason. Cuts wood well and aluminum kinda ok. Not for use with harder metals.
- **Ryobi wired circular saw**. Lives in the black bin things on the big red shelves.
- **Ryobi wireless circular saw**. Also lives in the black bin things on the big red shelves.
- **Ryobi wired jigsaw.** Lives in the black bin things on the big red shelves.
- **Scary old wired power hand planer.** It's not very well aligned and can be stuck in the on position before being plugged in. Maybe don't use it. Is this technically a saw?
- **Drill press.** Lives next to the mini-lathe. I'm calling this a saw for categorization purposes.

## 2D Printers
- **Dell 2155cn color laser printer.** It's a decent workhorse printer. If you print an image to it from linux it will print your image 3 times side-by-side in decreasing widths and quality. When this happens, you may wobble the laminated paper creature on the side of the PPE cabinet.
- **Epson ET-3850 EcoTank inkjet printer.** Decent quality inkjet usually used for printing stickers. Ecotank refils are on the shelf next to it.
- **Epson ST-2000 EcoTank inkjet modified for dyesub.** Don't use this for normal printing. Do use it for fancy dyesub printing.
