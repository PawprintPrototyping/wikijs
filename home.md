# Pawprint Prototyping Wiki

Welcome to the Pawprint Wiki. Here you'll find documentation about our tools, how the space operates, and knowledge contributed by enterprising members.

## Getting Started

- [Welcome](/welcome) — First-day orientation: Wi-Fi, parking tickets, HVAC, safety, and finding your way around.
- [Becoming a Member](/becoming-a-member) — What you need to join: dues, the membership agreement, and getting signed up.
- [Member Handbook](/member-handbook) — Our governance principles, rules, code of conduct, and how decisions get made — all in one place.
- [Space Switch](/space-switch) — What it means when the space is "open," and your responsibilities if you flip the switch.
